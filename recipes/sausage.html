Sausage And Roasted Peppers In The Oven

Is it too cold out to make sausages on the grill? Don't fry them in a pan, try cooking them in the oven! They cook more evenly and will be pretty close in texture and flavor to grilled sausage.

Ingredients

  • 1 package of your favorite raw sausage
  • Mini sweet peppers
  • Long buns
  • Non-stick cooking spray
  • (optional)Horseradish Mustard

Directions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 425Â°. Place sausages and mini sweet peppers on a pan that's been lightly spray with non-stick cooking spray while the oven heats.
  2. Once the oven is heated, place the pan with the sausages and peppers in to the oven then set the timer for 26 minutes. At 13 minutes, flip all food items on the pan and place back into the oven.
  3. After the timer goes off, pull the pan out of the oven and let it cool for 3-5 minutes
  4. Put the sausage and peppers into a bun with some mustard then enjoy it!
