Chicken Spinach Mushroom Fettucini Alfredo

This is a quite simplistic recipe for those of you who want something tasty but not too difficult after a hard day of work.

Ingredients

  • 1 jar of your favorite alfredo sauce
  • 3 medium size chicken breasts
  • 1 box of fettucini noodles (16oz)
  • 8oz of fresh sliced baby bella mushrooms
  • 6oz of spinach
  • 2 tablespoons of sunflower oil
  • 1 tablespoon of iodized salt
  • 3.5 quarts of water
  • Cavender's Greek Seasoning

Directions

Place the 3 medium-size chicken breasts in a lightly oiled glass dish and pre-heat the oven to 425°. While you wait for the oven to heat, add a bit of Cavender's Greek Seasoning on top of the breasts. Once the oven is heated to 425°, put the glass dish with the chicken breasts in the oven and set a timer for 23-26 minutes.

While the chicken is baking, fill a large pot with 3.5 quarts of water and 1 tablespoon of salt. Bring to a boil and add in the fettucini noodles. Cook the noodles for 11 minutes, then drain the water. Add a tablespoon of sunflower oil to the drained noodles and stir. The oil will keep the noodles from sticking together.

In a large pan, add a tablespoon of sunflower oil and get it heated up. Add the spinach and mushrooms, cook on a medium heat until the mushrooms are a little soft. Add in alfredo sauce and cut chicken breasts, simmer for 2-3 minutes.

Now that everything is finished, reap your reward and enjoy your easily made chicken alfredo!
