Protein Oats with Oat Milk

Oats are an easy and healthy food to make for the fitness enthusiast. Eat oats, make gains!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of old fashioned oats
  • 1 big scoop of peanut butter
  • 1 cup of oat milk
  • 1 banana
  • salt

Directions

  1. Measure out 1 cup of oats and 1 cup of oat milk. Pour both into a small non-stick pot. Add a dash of salt, then boil for 5 minutes or until the oats have absorbed all the oat milk.
  2. Pour cooked oats into a bowl. Grab a regular old spoon and scoop out a big glob of peanut butter and add it to the oats. Stir it in nice and good until it's consistent throughout.
  3. Cut up a banana with a butter knife and add to the oats.
